# Demo — integer & topological depth

> **Run:** `python examples/depth/depth_demo.py`

Shows the deeper layers driven by one exact Smith-Normal-Form engine:

- **modular conservation laws** — the torsion of `coker(S)`: structure invisible
  over ℚ (parity, CRT-fused residues);
- **integral homology** — Betti numbers + torsion of a chain complex and of the
  reconciliation **overlap nerve** (validated on `S¹`, `S²`, a torsion space).
